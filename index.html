Avatar Aang

The Avatar The Last AirBender.

The world is divided into four nations -- the Water Tribe, the Earth Kingdom, the Fire Nation and and the Air Nomads.

Each represented by a natural element for which the nation is named. Benders have the ability to control and manipulate the element from their nation.

Only the Avatar is the master of all four elements.

The ruthless Fire Nation wants to conquer the world but the only bender who has enough power, the Avatar, has disappeared ... until now.

Mastery of Elements

  • Air Bending
    • Airbenders are able to jump high and far by riding on strong gusts of wind and can also slow or deflect falls by creating cushions of air.
  • Water Bending
    • Waterbenders deal with the flow of energy; they let their defense become their offense, turning their opponents' own forces against them
    • The mark of a master waterbender is the ability to swiftly change from one state of water to another and rapidly switch techniques during combat, keeping the opponent off guard while constantly adapting to the enemy
  • Earth Bending
    • Earthbending, one of the four elemental bending arts, is the geokinetic ability to manipulate earth and rock in all their various forms, prevalent in the Earth Kingdom and the United Republic.
    • Earth is the element of substance, while the people of the Earth Kingdom are diverse, strong, and enduring.
